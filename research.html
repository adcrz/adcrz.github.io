Main Interests

I am interested in geometric measure theory/fractal geometry and harmonic analysis. Most questions I think about involve a fractal type set, the size of said set and what sort of patterns they contain. On the harmonic analysis side I like looking at the different techniques used to address problems in fractal geometry.
